Student Support Office

  1. Bydgoszcz
  2. Student Support Office

The Student Support Office is the first place you should turn to when facing challenges during your studies. The Student Support Office is dedicated to ensuring a smooth, friendly, and well-supported academic journey for international students. We strive to make students feel part of the university community, regardless of their country of origin, native language, or prior knowledge of the Polish education system. Our office is a space of trust where students can find not only information but also emotional support. We act as a bridge between students and the university administration, helping to overcome language, cultural, and procedural barriers.

Mission

Our mission is to provide a safe, inclusive, and supportive environment for every international student. We believe that effective support in the early stages contributes not only to academic success but also to personal growth.

Our goals include:

  • Providing students with the information needed to study and live confidently within the university system
  • Promoting intercultural understanding, tolerance, and respect for diversity
  • Ensuring equal opportunities for all students, regardless of their background, language, or experience
  • Building a community of acceptance and mutual support

The Student Support Office offers comprehensive assistance to international students during their time at the university. We provide support in:

  • Adapting to the new academic and cultural environment
  • Communicating with university administration (Dean’s Office, lecturers)
  • Assisting with the use of platforms such as ISAPS, ONTE, and MS Teams
  • Resolving organizational matters related to the study process
  • Providing information about schedules, documents, and student IDs
  • Explaining internal university procedures
  • Campus orientation and guidance for newly arrived students
  • Contact support via email and phone
