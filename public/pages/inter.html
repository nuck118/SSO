Student Support Office

  1. Bydgoszcz
  2. Student Support Office

The Student Support Office is your first place to go when you face difficulties during your studies. The Student Support Office dedicated to ensuring that international students experience a smooth, welcoming, and well-supported academic journey. We strive to help students feel like part of the university community, regardless of their country of origin, native language, or prior knowledge of the Polish education system. Our office is a space of trust where students can find not only information but also emotional support. We act as a bridge between students and university administration, helping to overcome language, cultural, and procedural barriers.

Whom do we support?

English-speaking students
Slavic-speaking students

Mission

Our mission is to provide a safe, inclusive, and supportive environment for every international student. We believe that effective early-stage support contributes not only to academic achievement but also to the student's personal development.

We aim to:

  • Provide students with the information they need to confidently study and live within the university system
  • Foster intercultural understanding, tolerance, and respect for diversity
  • Ensure equal opportunities for all students regardless of their background, language, or experience
  • Build a community of acceptance and mutual

The Student Support Office provides comprehensive assistance to international students during their stay at the university. We offer support in:

  • Adapting to a new academic and cultural environment
  • Communicating with university administration (Dean’s Office, lecturers)
  • Help in using ISAPS, ONTE, MS Teams platforms
  • Solving organizational matters related to the study process
  • Providing information on schedules, documents, and student ID cards
  • Explaining internal university procedures
  • Campus orientation and guidance for newly arrived students
  • Contact support via email, phone,
  • Academic Support – Help with coursework, tutoring, exam prep
  • Counseling & Mental Health – Confidential support and well-being services
  • Financial Aid & Scholarships – Guidance on funding, grants, and emergency aid
  • Career Services – Internships, CV help, job opportunities
  • Student Life & Engagement – Events, clubs, and everything that makes campus fun
  • Support for International Students – Orientation, housing, visa help, and more

Student Support Services

We're here to help you make the most of your university life — both inside and outside the classroom. Whether you're dealing with academic challenges, personal matters, or just need someone to talk to, we've got your back.
